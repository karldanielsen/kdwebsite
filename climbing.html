Joe's Valley: Finally Bouldering!
Joe's was my first day bouldering on a weeklong climbing trip, and it hit me like water in a desert.

The super tacky sandstone of the riverbank lines kept me entertained all day, even if all I did was punt off hard stuff.

No send on Big Joe, or the classic v8 in riverside despite best efforts.
